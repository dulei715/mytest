二. 托福阅读背诵

	1. Earth\'s internal heat, fueled by radioactivity, provides the energy for plate tactonics and continental drift, mountain building, and earthquakes.
	
	2. It can also be harnessed to drive electic generators and heat homes.
	
	3. Geothermal energy becomes available in a practical form when underground heat is transferred by water that is heated as it passes through a subsurface
	      region of hot rocks that may be hundreds or thousands of feet deep.
		  
	4. The water is usually naturally occurring groundwater that seep down along fractures in the rock; less typically, the water is artificially introduced
	      by being pumped down from the surface.
		  
	5. The water is brought to the surface, as a liquid or steam, through holes drilled for the purpose.
